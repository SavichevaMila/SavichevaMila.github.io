This Idaho Town - Preston

By Lyudmila Savicheva

Preston is a good place to live and spend the weekend. There are lots of great stuff for people to do there. You can do any outdoor activity during winter time such as: snowmobiling, skiing, snowboarding. This is one of the best place for winter break. There is a few activities you can do during summer time: water-skiing, hunting, fishing.

Cultural experience: - Festival of Lights. You can visit during thanksgiving week and Christmas. - Preston Rodeo night. In the beginning of august ( Thursday through Saturday). It is also callled The famous Preston night rodeo. During the event you can do shopping (There are antique shops, clothing boutiques, craft and collectable establishments, general merchandise and convenience stores).
